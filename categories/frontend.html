Frontend

Driven by the rapid development of the mobile Internet, the number of mobile-side development tasks is relatively large. With the implementation of the 5G standard, the development tasks of mobile terminals will be further expanded in the future; big data presentation is mainly based on the existing platform to complete the presentation of the final analysis results, and there are usually multiple choices for presentation methods, such as large-screen display.
