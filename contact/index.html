Photography talk

Throughout the history of photography, the art of photography has never been more approachable than in the current digital age. Whether online or in print, the number of photographs has grown unimaginably, and it is increasing every hour. The use of the camera is much more convenient than before, the quality of digital documents it can provide is getting higher and higher, and the range of captured images has become wider, and the shooting methods have never been more colorful. At the same time, photography-related courses offered by universities and colleges have continued to grow in recent years, and more and more books, DVDs, and even online tutoring guides are providing detailed practical tutoring to the public.

The existence of all these photography activities raises questions for us. What is the root cause of the public's desire for photos? What is the driving force for photographers to take pictures? What is driving them and what kind of goals do they want to achieve?
