Travel

  • Seattle

    Seattle is located in the northwest corner of Washington state between Puget Sound and Lake Washington. On the west side is the Olympic Mountains on the bay side, and on the east side is Lake Washington.

    To the east is Lake Shanmanmis, Isa Crossing the Highlands and Tiger Mountain. To the southeast are the famous Rainier Snow and Cascade Mountains. The coordinates of the city center are 47 ° 37'35''N and 122 ° W 19'59 ''. Seattle covers an area of 369.2 square kilometers, of which land area is 217.2 square kilometers, water area is 152 square kilometers, and water area rate is 41.16%.
